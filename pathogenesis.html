    You will play as a virus whose only option to go through this world that feels alive on its own is to infect all of its inhabitants.
    These are organisms that will try to destroy anything in its paths with their unique abilities. Possess them and use their skills to overcome all puzzles and dangerous scenarios in your way.

    DESCRIPTION

    Pathogenesis is a puzzle platformer game with pixel-art style made in Unity. Players will embark on an adventure surviving in an alive world where there is a threat in every organism they face there.

    These organisms are the core mechanic of the game. Our protagonist, a virus born from an amalgamation of the remians of other organisms, is not capable of going through the gloomy and fleshy dangers of this world on its own. That is why it needs to take advantage of the abilities of its enemies, using them to overcome many puzzles and obstacles.

    In Pathogenesis, we aim for a new type of environment that we call gruesome ASMR. Players will find a relaxing game but with gore elements. Our plans for the future with this game is to expand the world so the players can get the full experience of this unique combination of feelings, exploring a world engulfed in flesh, bones and blood and discovering ingenious puzzles.

    I had the chance to work not only as a game programmer (implementing AI, interactable features, game systems and SFX), but also as the main producer of the team. I organized the team, the meetings, all the tasks to do (using HacknPlan), contacted some outsourcing and onboarded them, as well as created some trackers.

    FEATURES

    The world of Pathogenesis is full of dangers. To overcome these obstacles, the virus has a precise movement and jump that allows it to get past most platforms. On top of that, thanks to its sliminess and viscosity, the virus can also go through a certain type of terrain filled with moving tentacles that will grab onto it, allowing for some incredible vertical movement defying gravity.

    As mentioned previously above, the rest of the organisms are some of the core mechanics. Each one of them has a special movement passive and active ability. If the protagonist can get close enough to get into contact with these organisms, it will possess them, gaining both of their abilities. This is crucial for solving puzzles and getting though certain terrain obstacles.

    The most interesting thing about these organisms is the dynamic they create between them:
    They serve as a rock/paper/scissors system, due to the fact that each one of them is strong against another organism, but weak against another one. This will also be the key of solving puzzles in many ocasions.
