    Work together to overthrow the revolution and claim back what is rightfully yours!

    DESCRIPTION

    Aard and Wyzz: The Rise of Minions is an action/adventure local coop game developed in Unreal Engine 5.2, where you will play as the two split personalities of the ruthless tyrant Ardwiz.
    Aard is the extrovert, brave, head-first-into-action part of Ardwiz. He represents his physical strength, hence his attacks are melee "magical" punches which the player will use to beat his enemies and move certain interactable items.
    Wyzz is the introvert, shy, witty and strategic part of the tyrant, so he prefers to stay far from the fight and use his magic to defeat the minions and use his range to solve distant pieces of puzzles.

    Both players will need to cooperate to ascend the Ardiwz Industries Inc. tower, claim back your rightful throne and merge back into the powerful Ardwiz. To do so, you will have to defeat different types of enemies, work together to solve puzzles and defeat powerful bosses that used to be head of their departments in your company, like Karen: Head of Finance.

    Take a look of the game and how to end the revolution in this trailer!

    FEATURES

    This game features a quite unique and funny setting that can place players in comical situations, but it also has distintive mechanics and features that can be enthralling.

    • As previously mentioned, you will have to choose to play Aard (melee combat) or Wyzz (ranged combat). Each character can use a quick sequence of attacks to do a combo with unique animations. If the attack button is held long enough, a heavy attack will be launched that will come in handy for a diverse variety of puzzles and help the players get the better of their enemies.

    • Before being able to go one floor upwards in this magical office tower, you will have to face a powerful and resourceful enemy: Karen, Head of Finance. She is the final boss of the current demo of the game, and serves as a final test for the cooperation of both players.

      Karen uses a complex AI system and certain algorithms to detect where players are located and their current actions, due to the fact that she will deliver different attacks depending if the players are solving some boss-fight puzzles or trying to dodge certain attacks.

    • There are various stages/phases in this boss fight. To be ablento defeat this formidable foe, players will need to make use of one of the most unique mechanics in the game: they will have to merge back into one, into Ardwiz.

      As the players used to play each half of the mighty Ardwiz, now, being whole again, each player will control certain part of Ardwiz's kit.
      The player who controlled Aard is in charge of moving Ardwiz and dashing away from all kinds of dangers. On the other hand, the one playing as Wyzz now controls his aim and his attacks (which consist in powerful shots and a destructive ray).

      Players are in dire need of communicating and cooperating more than ever, because they have to play as one single mind.

    • The protagonists will face various types of enemies that use different AIs and behaviour trees. To balance the fights for both players, enemies will go for the player with "the least amount of enemies" going for them, following an advanced aggro system.

      All enemies also know how to reposition accordingly after attacking, getting flanked or being attacked.

    • I also got to learn how to make shaders from scratch, like a shader that represents how damaged an enemy is, a dissolve shader for enemies dying and another dissolve shader for some destructible vines.
      On top of that, I implented 120+ SFX and many soundtracks with a certain adaptative factor (when going into conmbat, the music changes) using the FMOD Studio software.

    • And much, much more! Like interactables, simulating physics for movable boxes (not using the Unreal built-in physics), "friendly fire" with unique animations, aiming-assitance system, dynamic camera and many VFX.
